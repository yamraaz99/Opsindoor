April 22, 2025

The
Provocation

In the serene valley of Pahalgam, a barbaric terror attack shook the conscience of the world. 26 innocent civilians—25 Indians and 1 Nepali—were murdered in cold blood after being segregated by religion.

The Resistance Front (TRF), an offshoot of the Pakistan-based Lashkar-e-Taiba, claimed responsibility within hours—not once, but twice.

"The barbarity displayed by terrorists in Pahalgam on April 22 had shocked the entire country and the world."

— Prime Minister Narendra Modi

The Decision

April 23, 2025

Cabinet Security Committee

The CCS convenes to determine India's response. A clear, strong, and resolute message was deemed essential.

April 25, 2025

UN Condemnation

The UN Security Council issues a statement condemning the Pahalgam attack "in the strongest terms."

May 6-7, 2025

Operation Launched

Operation Sindoor commences. Indian Armed Forces conduct precision strikes against nine terrorist infrastructure sites.
